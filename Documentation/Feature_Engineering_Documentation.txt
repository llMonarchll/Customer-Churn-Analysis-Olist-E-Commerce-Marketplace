# Feature Engineering Documentation

Custom analytical features were created to transform transactional order-level data into customer-level datasets suitable for churn and retention analysis.

## Customer Churn Table (customer_churn_new)

### Customer Tenure

Calculated the number of days between the customer's first purchase and the maximum order date available in the dataset.

Purpose:

* Measure customer lifecycle length
* Segment customers by tenure

### Inactive Days

Calculated the number of days between the customer's most recent order and the maximum order date in the dataset.

Purpose:

* Measure customer inactivity
* Support churn classification

### Churn Flag

Customers with inactive_days > 180 were classified as churned.

Values:

* 1 = Churned
* 0 = Active

Purpose:

* Primary retention metric used throughout the analysis

### Churn Status

Business-friendly label derived from churn_flag.

Values:

* Churned
* Active

### Customer Type

Classified customers based on purchase frequency.

Values:

* One-Time Customer
* Repeat Customer

Purpose:

* Compare retention patterns between customer segments

### Revenue Bucket

Customers segmented according to total lifetime revenue.

Buckets:

* 0-100
* 101-299
* 301-499
* 501-1000
* 1000+

Purpose:

* Analyze churn across customer value segments

### Revenue Bucket Sort

Custom sort column created to maintain logical bucket ordering in Power BI visuals.

### Tenure Bucket

Customers segmented according to tenure duration.

Buckets:

* 0-89
* 91-119
* 121-179
* 181-364
* 365+

Purpose:

* Evaluate churn behavior across lifecycle stages

### Tenure Bucket Sort

Custom sort column created to maintain proper tenure bucket ordering.

### Order Frequency Bucket

Customer segmentation based on total number of orders.

Purpose:

* Compare churn across purchasing frequency groups

## Customer Experience Table (customer_experience)

### Delivery Days

Calculated as:

Delivery Date - Purchase Date

Purpose:

* Measure overall delivery performance

### Late Delivery Flag

Values:

* 1 = Delivered after estimated delivery date
* 0 = Delivered on or before estimated delivery date

Purpose:

* Measure delivery reliability

### Processing Days

Calculated as:

Order Approved Date - Purchase Date

Purpose:

* Measure seller processing performance

### Processing Days Bucket

Buckets:

* 0-2 days
* 3-5 days
* 6-8 days
* 9-12 days
* 12+ days

Purpose:

* Analyze customer experience by processing efficiency

### Processing Bucket Sort

Custom sort column created to maintain logical bucket ordering.

---

## Customer First Experience Table (customer_first_experience)

### First Delivery Days

Delivery duration associated with a customer's first completed order.

Purpose:

* Evaluate first-order fulfillment experience

### First Late Delivery Flag

Values:

* 1 = First order delivered late
* 0 = First order delivered on time

Purpose:

* Assess relationship between first-order delivery performance and future churn

### First Review Score

Customer review score associated with the first completed order.

Purpose:

* Evaluate relationship between initial satisfaction and retention
